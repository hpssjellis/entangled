Removes a list of neighbors from the connected IRI node.

Assumes removeNeighbors command is available on the node. This method has temporary effect until your IRI node relaunches.

The URI format:

  • udp://IPADDRESS:PORT
  • tcp://IPADDRESS:PORT

Parameters
[in]serviceclient service
[in]reqList of URIs that you want to remove from the node.
[out]resNumber of neighbors that were removed.
